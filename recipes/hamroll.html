The Ham Roll

My boyfriend says that these are gross, but he has yet to experience them. Don't knock it 'till you try it!

What you will need:

  • ham slices
  • mayonaise
  • optional: toppings of your choice! (example:cheetos, peppers, etc.)

Time to assemble!

  1. Apply a generous coat of mayonaise to one side of a ham slice.
  2. If you opted to include additional toppings, sprinkle them in now!
  3. Roll up your ham slice so that the mayonaise is contained within the ham tube!
!!!You can roll it up the opposite way if your feeling silly! Just mind the mess!!!

A note from the author:

I recommend you close your eyes as you take your first bites. Focus on the orchestra of textures!

Enjoy! You're welcome!
